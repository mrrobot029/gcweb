Undergraduate Studies

Admission Requirements
Freshmen:
  • Gordon College Admission Test (GCAT)
  • 2x2 ID Picture (white background)
  • Original F-138 (Report Card)
  • Good Moral Character Certificate
  • PSA Authenticated Birth Certificate
  • Medical Certificate issued by the College Clinic
Transferees:
  • GCAT (Admission Test)
  • 2x2 ID Picture (white background)
  • Official Transcript of Records (from the last University/College attended)
  • Certificate of Eligibility to Transfer /Transfer Credential or Honorable Dismissal
  • Good Moral Character Certificate
  • PSA Authenticated Birth Certificate
  • Medical Certificate issued by the College Clinic
Enrolment Procedures
  1. Secure registration form for Entrance Exam at the admission office
  2. Present duly accomplished registration form to the cashier and pay the exam fee
  3. Attach the official receipt to the form and return to the admission office for the entrance exam schedule
  4. Take the Admission Test on specified date
  5. Claim the entrance exam result and undergo career counselling
  6. Proceed to respective college for interview, submit requirements and get class schedule
  7. Proceed to the encoding and assessment section
  8. Proceed to the cashier for payment
  9. Report to the ID Personnel

Senior High School

Admission and Enrolment Procedure for Incoming Grade 11
  1. PRINCIPAL'S OFFICE (Ground Floor)
    • Fill-out Student's Information Profile

  2. CASHIER (Ground Floor)
    • Payment for Entrance Examination

  3. GUIDANCE OFFICE (3rd Floor)
    • Entrance Examination

  4. PRINCIPAL’S OFFICE (Ground Floor)
    • Interview by the Discipline Coordinator and Assistant Principal

  5. PRINCIPAL’S OFFICE (Ground Floor)
    • Submit the Complete Admission Requirements
      • NSO/PSA Certified Birth Certificate (Original & Photocopy)
      • Medical Certificate (Please inquire at the College Clinic at 3rd Floor)
      • Junior High School Report Card (F138)
      • Junior High School Certificate of Completion
      • Good Moral Character Certificate
      • NCAE result (For STEM Students ONLY)
      • ESC and SHS Voucher Certification (Students from Private Schools ONLY)
    • Secure and Fill-out Trial Form

  6. REGISTRAR’S OFFICE (Ground Floor)
    • Present Trial Form for Encoding and Assessment

  7. CASHIER (Ground Floor)
    • Pay the necessary fee(s) at the Cashier Windows.
    • The staff issues Student’s Copy of Enrollment Form (E-Form) and Official Receipt

  8. MIS OFFICE
    • Present E-Form and Official Receipt for ID Processing

  9. ROOM 202
    • Payment and Claiming of Books, Uniform and ID Lace

Tuition is FREE for Grade 10 Completers from Public Schools and Completers from Private Schools with ESC or SHS Voucher Certificate
